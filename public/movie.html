The Best Movies According to Diana

My top 5 movies for all moods

#1 The Deep House

A good horror about two YouTubers who specialize in underwater exploration.

#2 The Queen's Gambit

Set during the Cold War era, orphaned chess prodigy Beth Harmon struggles with addiction in a quest to become the greatest chess player in the world. It's a TV Show, has only 7 episodes but definitely worth to watch!

#3 American Pie (all parts!)

A riotous and raunchy exploration of the most eagerly anticipated -- and most humiliating -- rite of adulthood, known as losing one's virginity. In this hilarious lesson in life, love and libido, a group of friends, fed up with their well-deserved reputations as sexual no-hitters, decide to take action.

In addition to life jokes, the movies also contain mind-blowing music.

#4 Seven Psychopaths

This is a dark, screwball comedy-drama that constantly surprises.

#5 The Neon Demon

If you give yourself over to The Neon Demon, let yourself be completely immersed in its hypnotic fever dream, it will stick with you, burrowed in your subconscious. It truly is a work of art.


Put on your comfiest clothes, and bring your favorite snacks. Enjoy!
